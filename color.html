Portrait using Colored Pencils

Creating a portrait with colour pencils is a popular and enjoyable art form that allows you to capture the likeness and character of a subject in a detailed and colourful way.

Why Coloured Pencil?
  • Colour pencils come in a variety of hues and tones that can be used to create delicate gradations and layers of colour, allowing you to build up a rich and textured portrait.
  • They are portable and do not require any additional equipment, making them an excellent choice for artists who want to make art on the go.
  • It can also be a rewarding and satisfying experience that allows you to express your creativity and capture the beauty and complexity of the human form.
How to use it
  • When creating a colour pencil portrait, pay close attention to the subject's details, such as the form of the face, the texture of the skin, and the colours and highlights of the hair and eyes.
  • It's also crucial to use a variety of colours in the image to create depth and dimension, as well as diverse methods like blending, layering, and burnishing to obtain the desired appearance.
  • Combining skin tones can be a difficult task. Every skin tone is slightly different from the next, and finding the right one typically takes combining many tones.
