Art of Indian team World Cup 2011

Everything about the 2011 World Cup was spectacular; it was the most cherished and memorable event in Indian history. What a magnificent occasion it was, that even words are inadequate to describe the incredible match that was played on April 2nd, 2011, when India defeated Sri Lanka by 6 wickets in the final, contested at Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai, India and yes, nobody will ever forget Mahendra Singh Dhoni's game-winning six.

All of the portraits are created using the same process, which is black paint on paper. This is more than just a work of art; it represents unity, friendship, and feeling.
